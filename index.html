    The National Basketball Association (NBA) is the premier professional basketball league in the world. Established in 1946, the league features 30 teams competing at the highest level of the sport.

    The NBA has a rich history with legendary players such as Michael Jordan, Kobe Bryant, and LeBron James shaping the game. The league is known for its intense rivalries, thrilling playoffs, and global fan base.
